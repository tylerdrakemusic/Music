# You Already Know — Storyboard

> **Concept:** A warmth-and-light love story. The video arcs from isolation/cold into 
> togetherness/warmth, mirroring the lyrics' reassurance theme. Visual palette shifts 
> from cool blue tones to warm golden/amber as commitment builds.

---

## Visual Theme Summary

| Element | Early Sections (Intro–V1) | Mid Sections (Chorus–V2) | Bridge–Final Chorus |
|---------|--------------------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| **Color** | Cool blues, soft white | Warm amber, golden hour | Rich gold, deep warm tones |
| **Lighting** | Low-key, window light | Practical lights, candles | Full golden / backlit flares |
| **Energy** | Still, contemplative | Movement, connection | Cinematic, sweeping |
| **Setting** | Solo / separate spaces | Coming together | United, open spaces |

| Shot # | Lyric | Visual | Camera |

|--------|-------|--------|--------|

## Concept Variations to Consider

### Option A: "Narrative + Performance" (Recommended)
Mix a storyline (two people, trust building) with band/artist performance shots. Classic music video structure. Versatile, works at any budget.

### Option B: "One-Take Feel"
Choreographed long takes or simulated one-takes through locations. High energy, impressive, but demanding to shoot.

### Option C: "Visual Poem"
No literal narrative — abstract visuals, dance, art direction, color/light storytelling. The lyrics carry the story; the video is pure mood.

### Option D: "Lo-Fi / DIY Authentic"
iPhone/handheld aesthetic, real locations, real moments. Leans into the song's sincerity. Very achievable, can be shot fast.
