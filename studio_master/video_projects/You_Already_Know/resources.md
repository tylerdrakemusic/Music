# Music Video Resources

## Storyboarding Tools

### Free / Low-Cost
- **Canva** — drag-and-drop storyboard templates, free tier available
- **Boords** (boords.com) — purpose-built storyboard app, free for 1 project
- **StudioBinder** (studiobinder.com) — free shot list & storyboard templates, industry-standard call sheet generator
- **ShotDeck** (shotdeck.com) — searchable database of movie frames for visual reference / inspiration
- **Milanote** (milanote.com) — visual mood boarding, free tier

### AI-Assisted
- **Midjourney / DALL-E** — generate concept frames for each storyboard panel (use your Midjourney profile code: `--p c33c891e-24ed-42f2-bd6f-6cb8d9c75083`)
- **Runway ML** (runwayml.com) — AI video generation for concept previsualization
- **Kaiber** (kaiber.ai) — AI music video generator (feed song + style prompt)
- **Pika Labs** — text-to-video for quick visual tests

## Pre-Production References

### Shot Composition & Camera
- **Every Frame a Painting** (YouTube) — visual storytelling breakdowns
- **Cinematography Database** (cinematographydb.com) — gear + settings for reference films
- **In Depth Cine** (YouTube) — practical music video lighting/camera tutorials

### Music Video Breakdowns
- Search YouTube: "music video breakdown" or "how ___ music video was made"
- **Genius "Verified"** series — sometimes covers video concepts
- **Directors cuts / BTS** on Vimeo from music video directors

### Color & Mood
- **MoviePalettes** — color palettes extracted from films
- **Coolors.co** — palette generator to lock your cool-to-warm gradient
- **Pinterest boards** — search "music video color grading warm" or "cinematic golden hour"

## Post-Production

### Editing Software
- **DaVinci Resolve** — free, professional-grade editing + color grading + effects
- **CapCut** — quick social media cuts, free
- **Premiere Pro** — industry standard, subscription
- **Final Cut Pro** — Mac only, one-time purchase

### Color Grading
- DaVinci Resolve has the best free color tools in the industry
- Search "cinematic warm color grade DaVinci" for tutorials matching this video's arc

### Effects
- **After Effects** — snow particles, light leaks, text
- **HitFilm** — free alternative to AE for compositing
