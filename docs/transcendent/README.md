# Transcendent — Tier Definition

Songs in this folder arrived **whole** — hooks, arc, and emotional truth intact in a single moment of inspiration.

They are not ideas to be merged or developed from scratch. They are **already themselves**. Development happens around what's here, not to what's here.

---

## Tier Hierarchy

| Tier | Location | What belongs there |
|------|----------|--------------------|
| **Raw ideas** | `f:\executedcode\lyrics\` | Fragments, lines, half-formed merges, word sketches |
| **Transcendent** | `docs/transcendent/` ← *this folder* | Complete hook visions, arrived whole in a flash |

---

## Rules for This Tier

1. **Capture verbatim first** — the exact words as they arrived go in, unedited.
2. **Analysis is additive** — thematic notes, structure ideas, and rhyme-family expansions are welcome but live below a clear separator. They do not overwrite the original hooks.
3. **No forced completion** — transcendent pieces are not assigned a deadline. When they're ready to become full songs, they move to the production pipeline (`albums`/`tracks` DB tables).
4. **One file per song vision** — filename matches the anchor hook or working title, kebab-case.
